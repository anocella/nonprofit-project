Our Goal

To add transparency to the nonprofit sector

The nonprofit sector currently stands at about 5% or so of US GDP and hasnâ€™t experienced any profound growth over the past several years. Donations are being scattered to many firms that support the same initiatives and firms are forced to squeeze their overhead costs until they are hardly able to operate (let alone expand their operations).


Nonprofit Credit Score was developed so donors could make more informed decisions when it comes to their donations. Our goal is to add more transparency to the nonprofit sectors by making it easy for anyone to interpret the financial health of a nonprofit organization by simply viewing their NPS. In addition, our hope is that the NPS will incentivize nonprofits to be more financially responsible.


Our NPS metric is calculated using an algorithm that takes into account the most important financial metrics from a nonprofit's Form 990, including operating reserve, growth in net assets, operating efficiency, and more.


We hope that you find Nonprofit Credit Score to be helpful in your decision-making process prior to donating. Making informed nonprofit decisions is one of the most important things donors can do in order to facilitate an efficient nonprofit sector.
